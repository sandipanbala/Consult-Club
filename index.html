  • July 2015-Present

    Creating Stability Lobes Diagram

    Guide: Prof. Ramesh Singh, IIT-Bombay

    A stability lobe diagram based on regenerative chatter theory can be a tool to optimize the cutting speeds and chip widths.the stability diagram is formed by the intersections of u shaped borderlines of stability.The intersections of the lobes denote the deepest stable cuts at various ranges of spindle speed.
    To get the curves we need to solve the equations of continuous chatter(vibrations) and then try for a graphical solution. Our aim in this project is to make this lengthy procedure of solving equations and getting lobes into a much simpler plug and play process.
    In this project we also want to consider the effect of tool geometry on the cutting process.we can observe the effect of rake angle and the radius of curvature of the cutting edge on the stability of the process.
